Visit us

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we are committed to making science accessible to all.

Opening Hours

  • Monday: Closed
  • Tuesday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Wednesday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Thursday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Friday: 10:00 – 19:00
  • Saturday: 9:00 – 16:00
  • Sunday: 9:00 – 13:00

Address

The museum is located at Birkelyveien 10.

Food and beverage

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our museumshop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
